Seam carver
Implementing this elegant photo-resizing algorithm was a programming assignment I completed with my partner Kathryn Leung for COS 226: Data Structures and Algorithms. It resizes photos by removing "seams" of least energy from the photo, preserving high-energy regions of the photos corresponding to important features, like faces.
